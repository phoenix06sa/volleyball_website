About The Club

Auburn Select Volleyball is a premier competitive club dedicated to the development of elite student-athletes. Founded on the principles of hard work and technical discipline, we focus on preparing players for the next level of competition.

Our philosophy is simple: we train the person, not just the player. Through rigorous practice and high-level tournament play, we build character, leadership, and volleyball IQ.
